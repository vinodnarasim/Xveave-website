AI Capabilities

Intelligence built into every stage of the design phase.

DesignVeave doesn't just capture — it actively assists. AI that works from your actual program data, not generic suggestions.

AI Feature 01

Design Solution Proposals

AI analyses your workshop notes and automatically proposes solutions — updating the Business Process Design with structured entries for solutions, configuration, and key decisions. Accept, review, or reject each proposal directly in the platform.

What would take hours of manual write-up happens in minutes — with full traceability back to the note that triggered it.

Agent Proposals — Accept, Review or Reject AI-generated design updates

Find Similar — 65% match from prior BPD, 8 items ready to copy

AI Feature 02

Discovery & Reusability

Before building anything new, DesignVeave searches your repository of prior BPDs for semantically similar designs — surfacing the best match with a relevance score and a summary of what overlaps.

Copy requirements, solutions, and decisions from a prior program directly into your current BPD. Your accumulated implementation experience becomes a living asset — not locked in old project files.

Proprietary Knowledge Base

Every program enriches your own BPD repository. Your firm's accumulated experience becomes a searchable, continuously augmented competitive asset.

Documentation Assistance

AI-assisted documentation refined for business audiences — outputs that stakeholders can read, review, and sign off on without needing a translator.

BPD Validation

Before submission to the business, DesignVeave validates the BPD for completeness — flagging gaps, missing decisions, and unresolved requirements before they become program issues.
