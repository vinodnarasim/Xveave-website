Innovations / SAP Implementation Accelerator

Accelerators & Frameworks Pilot engagements

Business process design, captured properly.

One structured workspace for the entire S/4HANA design phase: workshop sessions, process documentation, requirements, decisions, and RICEF scope — all linked and traceable from discovery through build.

The problem

Implementations run on workshops. Workshops run on memory.

S/4HANA implementations are shaped in design workshops — and the decisions made in them usually end up scattered across slide decks, meeting notes, and people's recollections. By the build phase, nobody can trace why a requirement exists, which workshop decided it, or whether a gap genuinely needs custom development.

The result is familiar to anyone who has run a transformation program: rework, scope disputes, and documentation debt that compounds until go-live.

Capabilities

From workshop to build, without losing the thread.

  • Workshop mode — live note capture, attendee tracking, and AI-generated meeting minutes.
  • Structured process documentation — organized by business area and process, not by file share.
  • Requirements & decision registers — every item traceable back to the workshop that produced it.
  • RICEF gap analysis & functional specs — spec authoring with reusable templates, linked to the gaps they close.
  • AI-powered discovery — find similar objects across the project before building new ones.
  • PM dashboards — project health, burn-up, and decision tracking at a glance.
Who it's for

Consulting teams and system integrators running S/4HANA implementation or transformation programs — and the project managers accountable for their outcomes.

Status

In active development with pilot engagements.
