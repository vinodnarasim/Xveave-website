Innovations / Selective Distribution Management

Industry Innovations In validation

Distribution intelligence where standard SAP stops.

Intelligent distribution capabilities that encode channel rules and partner logic for selective and multi-tier distribution models — integrated with the SAP core.

The problem

Selective distribution lives in spreadsheets and tribal knowledge.

Fashion and retail brands running selective or multi-tier distribution face channel rules, partner eligibility, and allocation decisions that standard SAP processes were never designed to handle. The logic that protects the brand ends up outside the system — fragile, manual, and invisible to the enterprise core.

Capabilities

Channel rules and partner logic, made systematic.

  • Partner & channel eligibility management — encode who may sell what, where, and under which conditions.
  • Allocation & assortment intelligence — distribution decisions informed by channel rules and demand.
  • Policy compliance & monitoring — visibility into distribution behavior against brand policy.
Who it's for

Fashion and retail organizations with selective or multi-tier distribution models running on an SAP core.

Status

In concept validation.
