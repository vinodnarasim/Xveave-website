Innovations / AI Development Platform

Accelerators & Frameworks POC complete

Your codebase, made reusable.

A RAG-powered platform that extracts, indexes, and semantically searches your existing custom code — then adapts the best match to new requirements instead of starting from zero.

The problem

Enterprises keep rebuilding what they already own.

Custom objects get rebuilt project after project, because nobody can find what already exists. Custom-code estates built over decades sit unindexed and unsearchable, while every new requirement starts from a blank page.

Capabilities

Search it, score it, reuse it.

  • Automated code extraction — pull custom code directly from S/4HANA systems.
  • Semantic matching — across tables, BAPIs, modules, and keywords, with weighted scoring so the best reuse candidate surfaces first.
  • AI-assisted spec parsing & adaptation — from specification to adapted code, grounded in your estate.
  • Grounded answers — results come from your code reality, not generic training data.
Who it's for

SAP development teams and AMS organizations with large custom-code estates; transformation programs assessing custom code ahead of S/4HANA moves.

Status

Proof of concept complete; available as a foundation for client-specific deployments.

Next step
